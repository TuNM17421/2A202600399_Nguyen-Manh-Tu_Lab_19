# xAI

**X.AI Corp.**, doing business as **xAI**, is an American company working in the area of artificial intelligence (AI), social media, and technology. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of American aerospace company **SpaceX**.

## Overview

Founded by **Elon Musk** with a team of 11 researchers in **2023**, the company's flagship products include:

- **Grok** — a generative AI chatbot
- **Grokipedia**
- **X** — the social network acquired by xAI in March 2025
