# OpenAI

**OpenAI Global, LLC** is an American artificial intelligence (AI) research organization consisting of a for-profit public benefit corporation (PBC) and a nonprofit foundation, headquartered in San Francisco.

OpenAI developed the **GPT** (generative pre-trained transformer) family of large language models, the **DALL-E** series of text-to-image models, and the **Sora** series of text-to-video models. Its release of **ChatGPT** in November 2022 has been credited with catalyzing widespread interest in generative AI.

## History & Structure

OpenAI was founded in **2015** in Delaware as a nonprofit. A for-profit subsidiary was created in **2019**, and a **2025** restructuring converted it into a PBC that is **26% owned** by the nonprofit. **Microsoft** previously invested over **$13 billion** into OpenAI and provides Azure cloud computing resources. In October 2025, OpenAI conducted a **$6.6 billion** share sale that valued the company at **$500 billion**.

## Controversies

- In **2023 and 2024**, OpenAI faced multiple lawsuits for alleged copyright infringement against authors and media companies whose work was used to train its products.
- In **November 2023**, OpenAI's board removed **Sam Altman** as CEO, citing a lack of confidence, but reinstated him five days later following a board reconstruction.
- Throughout **2024**, roughly half of then-employed AI safety researchers left OpenAI, citing the company's deprioritization of safety goals.
