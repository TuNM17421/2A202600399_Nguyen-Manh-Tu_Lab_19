# Anthropic

**Anthropic** is an American artificial intelligence (AI) company headquartered in San Francisco. It has developed a range of large language models (LLMs) named **Claude** and focuses on **AI safety**.

## Overview

Anthropic was founded in **2021** by former members of OpenAI, including siblings **Daniela Amodei** and **Dario Amodei**, who serve as president and CEO, respectively. The company is privately held and, as of February 2026, had an estimated value of **$380 billion**.
