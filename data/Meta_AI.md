# Meta AI

**Meta AI** is a research division of **Meta** (formerly Facebook) that develops artificial intelligence and augmented reality technologies.

## Key Relationships

- **Meta AI** is part of **Meta Platforms**.
- **Meta AI** develops **Llama** models.
- **Meta's Llama 3.1** is an open-weight large language model developed by **Meta**.
- **Meta AI** focuses on **Artificial Intelligence** and augmented reality.
- **Meta** uses technology **open-weight models** for its Llama series.
- **Meta AI** is the AI research arm of **Meta Platforms, Inc.**
